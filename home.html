Ala-Too International University (AIU) was established in 1996 and it is located in Bishkek, the Kyrgyz Republic. There are 4 faculties, 3 institutes and 16 departments in AIU. The main language of instruction is English. The staffing of the AIU is composed of highly qualified, experienced, young, active teachers from Kyrgyzstan, Turkey, USA, China, India and other countries.
